- **Squad quality gap.** Oranje core intact: Van Dijk, Frenkie de Jong, Gravenberch, Gakpo, Dumfries, Depay. Multiple sources frame the talent edge as decisive (@Lummox_eth: "the line up is better: Gakpo, Dumfries, De Jong").

## Why I'm NOT confident in the result (consequence — low confidence)

- **It's one match.** ~25% means Japan win outright once in every four playings of this fixture. That is not a tail risk.
- **Japan's giant-killing record is real, not romance.** Beat Germany and Spain at the 2022 World Cup; arrived on six straight wins including Brazil and England. The upset case (@tueateind: "my pick: 2-1 Japan"; @_Pretty_Miraa: "Japan feels like the live underdog") is grounded.

- **The draw is priced high (~27%)** for a reason — a cagey, low-event 1-1 is very much in play and was a popular fan prediction (@chidowrld: 1-1).
