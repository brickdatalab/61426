## THE WINNER (passes every criterion, including LOBO on all 52 subsets)

**BAFO — book-against flow override.** Exact rule: when the smoothed book (`imbEwma`) sign OPPOSES the cushion sign, AND `|cushion| ≥ max(30, 3.0 × vol_1m)`, AND `sign(d60) == sign(cvd_d3m) == sign(cushion)` → the entry candidate becomes the cushion direction (normal momentum fold and 7-tick dwell still apply).

|---|---|---|---|
| correct fires | 5,480 | **5,962** | **+482 (+8.8%)** |
| wrong fires | 1,926 | **1,917** | **−9** |
| missed fires | 3,966 | **3,493** | **−473** |

Generalization: 7 distinct bars improved (across v5.1-era, v5.2-era and v5.3-era sessions; largest single-bar share of the gain 28%), all trend-class — consistent with the rule's claim: it rescues strong trends that the order book fights the whole way (the bar-85200 failure shape). It fires nothing new in chop or late-flip bars, and costs nothing there.

## REJECTED — with the number that killed each (no maybes)

- **DWELL 7→3 and 7→5** (agent XVAL's top static pick, predicted 7.5:1 win): dynamic replay refuted it — correct −2.6%, wrong +137, transitions 9.12 avg/16 max, **20 bars hurt >10%**. The static analysis ignored that a shorter dwell admits every transient candidate. REJECTED.
- **LP_DROP / FAKE_CORR (the inverted-corroborator suppressors):** the aggregate finding is real — lp-corroborated counter-cushion fires run 9–14% accurate overall, and these rules remove 615–676 wrong ticks — but they erase **100% of the correct ticks on 3 late-flip bars** (27→0, 34→0, 43→0) and convert +66 correct→wrong on a fourth. Counter-cushion fires are how flips get caught; killing the class kills the catches. FAILS the no-bar-hurt criterion. REJECTED as shipped rules (the aggregate inversion finding stands and is recorded for a future, smarter treatment).
- **DZ_RESCUE / DUAL_CORR / HZ10 stacks** (up to +1,168 correct, −551 wrong in the biggest stack): every stack fails leave-one-bar-out on the wrong-not-increased criterion by **1–3 ticks** (their tiny per-bar wrong additions lose their offset when a wrong-reducing bar is left out). Under the approved contract these are REJECTED. They are the strongest near-misses; re-test when the dataset doubles.
- **CC_RATIO, DECAY_EXIT, PENDING_LEAN, FLOWLED_DZ:** each fails ≥2 criteria (bar-hurt and/or wrong increased and/or transitions); exact numbers in `scratchpad` sweep results (`res_lhf_singles.json`, `res_lhf_combos.json`, `res_lhf_final.json`).

## Evidence-agent findings that informed the sweep (all counts from the ledger)

- Wrong fires concentrate in counter-cushion states: aligned fires run 55–95% accurate; lp-"corroborated" counter fires run 13.7% (691 wrong / 110 correct ticks). Momentum is FLAT on 95% of counter-cushion ticks (it corroborates almost nothing).
- Missed-fire cost by blocking gate: counter-blocked 1,364 ticks · dead-zone 1,280 · book-against 661 (top 3 = 83.4% of all misses). BAFO addresses the book-against slice; the other two remain the largest future opportunities.
- State-quality (extends the 58%/91% finding): run ≥31 aligned accuracy by cushion ratio: 63.5% (<0.5×) → 55.3% (0.5–1×) → 78.9% (1–2×) → 95.0% (≥2×).
- Taxonomy: 23 trend / 17 chop / 12 late-flip bars. Late-flip bars produce wrong ticks at 3.3× the trend rate; trend bars hold 58% of all missed ticks.

## Closing caveat (the one directional statement)

Every number above is a measured count on these 52 verified bars, and the winner's stability was tested on every 51-bar subset — that is the strongest generalization test this data supports. Performance in future market regimes cannot be measured in advance; the v5.4-vs-v5.3 live comparison (`_v54` logs) is the ongoing check.
