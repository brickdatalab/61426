# V5.3 Design — Max-Accuracy Signal Profile

**Date:** 2026-07-02 · **Base:** fork of **v5.1** (user decision — not v5.2; no localStorage prune, no authed-route repoint; POSTs to the legacy open `/log` with a `_v53` slug suffix). First approved signal-logic change since v5.1 froze.

## Goal

Fire the signal tag with the highest conviction: fewest wrong calls, correct calls held decisively. Chosen by the user from three measured profiles (balanced / max-accuracy / max-coverage): **Max accuracy.**

## Profiles considered (counterfactual replay over the 20 verified tuning bars)

| profile | acc | coverage | trans/bar | wrong eps | first-fire |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| v5.1 baseline | 70.8% | 50.6% | 5.85 | 26 | 70% |
| **Max accuracy (CHOSEN)** | **80.1%** | 57.1% | 5.10 | 17 | 65% |
| Balanced flagship | 77.5% | 82.3% | 2.40 | 13 | 55% |
| Max coverage | 76.0% | 87.4% | 3.00 | 20 | 55% |

## The three rules (all inside `decideDebounced`; everything else identical to v5.1)

1. **Cushion-aligned entry (`ALIGNED_ENTER: 0.14`)** — the entry threshold drops 0.20→0.14 only for the direction agreeing with the cushion sign. Recovers hold-zone misses on genuine moves.
2. **Counter-cushion confirmation** — a NEW entry against the cushion requires corroboration (momentum agrees OR whale-print sign agrees), else hold. Kills the dominant failure class (18/26 wrong episodes were uncorroborated counter-entries at 33% accuracy).
3. **Hold-release (`HOLD_RELEASE: 15`)** — a HELD state against the cushion with no momentum/whale backing for 15 consecutive ticks decays to MIXED (dwell-debounced). Discovered from bar `btc-updown-5m-1782985200` (288/295 wrong DOWN ticks on an UP bar — book decoupled from price all bar). Measured: zero effect on the 20 tuning bars (never triggers); out-of-sample accuracy 58.1%→75.2% (n=6); bar-85200 wrong ticks 288→67.

## Measured expectations (scratchpad harness, proven tick-identical to the module)

- Tuning split (20 bars): acc 80.1 / cov 57.1 / trans 5.10 / wrongEp 17.
- Out-of-sample split (6 bars, incl. 85200): acc 75.2 vs v5.1's 45.4. **n=6 — directional, not calibrated.**

## Acceptance gate (STOP condition)

`v5.3/analysis/replay-compare.mjs` replays v5.3 vs v5.1 over ALL settled logs. Ship only if: tuning split within ±3pts of the numbers above (else port bug), AND out-of-sample acc ≥ v5.1 with wrongEp ≤ v5.1 and transitions avg ≤6 / max ≤9. The flip-bar TRUE-POSITIVE alert fixture test must pass unchanged.

## Non-goals (explicitly excluded)

- No `W_BIN` book re-weighting, no ALPHA/DWELL/EXIT changes (different profile, not chosen).
- No flip-score / alert changes (alert layer already nailed bar 85200 with 4.7min lead — untouched).
- No dashboard feature changes beyond the `_v53` namespace; no VM changes.

## Provenance honesty

`ALIGNED_ENTER` and `HOLD_RELEASE` are FIT to captured data (20 tuning bars; hold-release found via one OOS bar and validated on 6). They are not untuned priors; the in-code comments say so. Re-validate as `_v53` logs accumulate.
