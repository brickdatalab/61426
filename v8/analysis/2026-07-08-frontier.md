# v8 frontier — walk-forward ceiling measurement (2026-07-08)

The Phase-A verdict for the v8 per-tick signal program. Everything below is
walk-forward (train earlier UTC days → test later days, never random), scored
under the anti-echo value rule (`30_scoring.py`: correct = +unpricedness×earliness,
wrong = −1, MIXED-during-fire-worthy-lead = −0.25). Evidence base: **823 cleaned
full-tick bars (Jul 5–8, 24/7), 48,557 sampled ticks, 40 features across 7
families** — including the never-before-used taker buy/sell split, side-split
whale flow, perp-spot basis, book bid/ask pull rates, poly dynamics, and VWAP.
Pooled held-out test: **603 bars / 35,577 ticks**. Regenerate: `40_ceiling.py`,
`41_policy.py` (seeded).

## 1. The headline

**No model and no feature family beats the plain cushion rule on the value
metric.** `sign(cushion)` gated by the vol floor (`|cushion| ≥ max($10, 0.5·vol_1m)`)
scores **−1.134/bar** on held-out data; the best 40-feature model under the
value-optimal dynamic-threshold policy reaches only −1.753 (logit) / −2.661 (hgb),
and even a cushion-only logit under that optimal policy (−1.499) loses to the raw
rule. Composite overlays (model veto on cushion calls, EV-positive fills in the
MIXED zone) improve it at most to **−1.102/bar (+3% relative)** — not worth a
40-weight model in a pure-rules engine.

Permutation importance says why: `cush_norm` 0.380, second place (`mid_cush_gap`,
itself half cushion) 0.022, everything else ≤0.006. Group-shuffle confirms TRAJ
(cushion trajectory) carries ~an order of magnitude more value than any
microstructure family. **The taker split, whale split, basis, book pulls, poly
dynamics, and VWAP add ≈ nothing predictive beyond price location** — the
per-tick equivalent of the v7 OOS finding (the market and the price already
contain the public tape).

## 2. What the frontier signal IS

The measured-best per-tick stream is embarrassingly simple and is a huge upgrade
over v6's lean stream on the identical held-out ticks:

| stream | value/bar | accuracy | coverage | wrong | missed (fire-worthy) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **cushion+floor rule (v8 candidate)** | **−1.134** | **82.3%** | 73.5% | 13.0% | **0.0%** |
| v6 lean stream (replayed, byte-faithful) | −6.527 | 73.7% | 59.9% | 15.8% | **21.3%** |
| poly sign | −3.537 | 75.4% | 83.9% | 20.6% | 10.8% |
| VWAP anchor | −5.654 | 78.1% | 56.4% | 12.4% | 18.1% |

Per rem-band accuracy of the candidate (823-bar full set): 67.4% @ 46.7% cov in
minute 1 → 72.8% → 78.8% → 85.9% → 92.3% @ 82.0% cov in minute 5.

It fixes both documented failures **by construction**:
- **MIXED-while-evidence-screams:** 30 pain-case bars found (|cushion|≥$150,
  ≥$1M same-side CVD, v6 = MIXED); the rule calls the evidence side on every one.
  Zero missed fire-worthy ticks, vs v6's 21.3%.
- **Untrustworthy first call:** the first call is no longer a book echo (v6 fires
  off raw order-book imbalance ~10s in, measured ~60–63%); it is a price-location
  call that only exists once a real vol-scaled lead exists, with honest per-band
  accuracy attached (67% in minute 1, rising monotonically).

## 3. Honest limits (unchanged physics)

- "Accurate before the market prices it in" has a measured wall: unpriced+early
  correctness is exactly what the value metric rewards, and **every** strategy
  scores negative on it — the market is calibrated; early unpriced certainty is
  not available from public data at scale. What IS available: never miss the
  developing move (0% missed), never echo noise (13% wrong, decaying to 6% late).
- ~8% near-flat oracle coin-flip bars cap everything (coin-flip-excluded
  sensitivity improves all numbers, ordering unchanged).
- Day-8 fold is small (36 bars); folds 1–2 (284+283 bars) dominate and agree.
- Book-pull family: levels were present and clean (100% valid) — measured, and
  still ≈0 lift. Deeper-book collection is NOT justified by this evidence.

## 4. Recommendation to the operator (Task 7 checkpoint)

Ship v8's per-tick stream as the **calibrated cushion-lead rule with hysteresis**
(dwell/exit tuned in dynamic replay — the static rule may flap at the floor
boundary), inheriting v7s's early-call channel unchanged. It is simple, fully
explainable, beats the incumbent stream by ~5.4 value points/bar and +8.6pp
accuracy with zero missed leads, and every number above is regenerable. The
40-feature model, the probabilities, and the composite overlays are preserved
under `v8/analysis/` for future regimes, but they do not earn a place in the
shipped engine today.
