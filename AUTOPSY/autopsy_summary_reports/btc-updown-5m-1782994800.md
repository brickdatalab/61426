### Why it was right when it was right

Two of the four RIGHT UP episodes (166→112 and 21→1) are the clean shape: a fat cushion (1.92× and 1.66× the vol floor) agreeing with book ewma, no corroborator gymnastics needed. The other two (268→257 and 57→43) were thin — 0.55× and 0.3× the vol floor, squarely in the measured 63–65% coin-flip zone — and got there right on aligned entries that happened to land on the correct side; those two are lucky, not structurally sound calls.

### Why it was wrong when it was wrong — the pattern, precisely

The one WRONG episode (289→269, DOWN) carries no `failure_flags` from the script, and it doesn't map to any of the documented classes: it isn't `inverted-whale-corroborator` (this was an aligned entry, not a counter-cushion one, and large_prints was flat/negative rather than corroborating a counter fire); it isn't `thin-aligned-vs-flow` (that class requires flow running opposite the call, but here d3m (−2.66M) and since_open (−192k) both agreed with the DOWN entry); it isn't a late-bar flag (this fired 20–70 seconds into the bar, not near close). What actually happened: book ewma (−0.44), a thin cushion (0.51× floor), and 3-minute flow all pointed DOWN together at rem 289, and the bar simply reversed anyway — cushion crossed to positive by rem 268 and stayed net-positive for 244 of 294 ticks. This is an unclassified early-bar whipsaw: every available voice agreed and was still wrong, which isn't the same failure as "wrong side outvoted the truth-teller" seen in the other autopsies — here there was no truth-teller yet to be outvoted, since the bar's real direction hadn't shown up until after this episode closed.

On the v5.4 side: the mirror is tick-identical except for the final 6 ticks (rem 6→1), where v5.3 correctly held UP and v5.4 drops to MIXED. That divergence doesn't fit the BAFO description in patterns.md — BAFO only adds fires *toward* a fat cushion when the book opposes it and flow agrees, it doesn't silence an existing aligned call. Whatever gate produced this isn't captured in the script's output, so it's an unexplained divergence, not a BAFO artifact: on this bar it cost v5.4 six ticks of a correct late fire without helping it.
